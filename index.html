I am an Associate Professor of Statistics at Temple University. My research focuses on empirical Bayes inference, multiple testing, sufficient dimension reduction, selection inference, and statistical application. I am currently serving as the Co-Editor-in-Chief of Communications in Statistics: Theory and Methods. I am an elected member of International Statistical Institute.

天普大学统计系副教授，研究领域包括经验贝叶斯、多重假设检验、充分降维理论及其方法，模型选择后的统计推断以及统计应用等。目前担任期刊 Communications in Statistics: Theory and Methods 的共同主编。

Curriculum Vitae Research Statement

Research Interests

  • High-Dimensional and Complex Data Analysis
  • Bayesian and Empirical Bayes Methods for Complex Models
  • Post-Selection Inference and Model Uncertainty
  • Simultaneous Inference and Multiple Testing in Large-Scale Settings
  • Modern Nonparametric and Distribution-Free Methods
  • Dimension Reduction and Representation Learning in High Dimensions
